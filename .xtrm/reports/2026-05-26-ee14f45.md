### Materializer chain (the work that landed)

- **apps/gitboard/src/core/materializer/index.ts** — runOnce/resync no longer hardcode substrate-issues writes; delegate to per-adapter `write()` (and `writeFull()` for resync after .70). `getCursor` wraps `JSON.parse` in try/catch and emits warn log on corrupt cursor. `bumpEpoch(repoSlug)` called after COMMIT for `obs:*` sources to invalidate specialists route cache. `materializer.run` log carries `duration_ms` + `rows_written` + `dependencies_written`. `SourceQueue.drain` catch emits `materializer.error` log (no more silent swallow).
